Kristina Ousley, Maker of Pretty Things

I’m a Creative Technologist who is passionate about blending innovative ideas with technical expertise to make magic happen. I am a two-time HBCU graduate currently finishing a Doctorate in Information and Interaction Design, where my research focuses on leveraging extended reality gaming to improve mental health. My areas of expertise are web design, app development, digital illustration, and project management. Whether it’s an app idea that needs to be brought to life, or a logo that perfectly defines your brand, or storyboarding for the next big PC game, I can craft it. When I’m not pushing the boundaries of my digital skills, I can be found coaching competitive cheerleading or bartending.
I am the mind behind the Walking Chandelier (TWC) is a conglomerate of businesses that foster creativity, community, and a commitment to a sustainable future. Notable work includes multimedia design for corporate entities, networking events, and education programs.
